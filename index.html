About the State

Ramrajya, a land steeped in ancient history and vibrant culture, offers a unique blend of heritage, nature, and spirituality. Its diverse landscapes range from lush green forests to serene riverbanks, each telling a story of its own. The state is renowned for its architectural marvels, spiritual centers, and a warm, hospitable populace that welcomes visitors with open arms.

Explore the intricate carvings of ancient temples, trek through untouched natural reserves, and immerse yourself in the local traditions and festivals. Ramrajya is more than a destination; it's an experience that stays with you forever.

District of Ayodhya

History & Culture

Ayodhya, the spiritual heart of Ramrajya, is a city of profound religious significance. It is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, a central figure in Hindu mythology. The city is adorned with magnificent temples, ghats, and a rich history that dates back millennia. Its culture is deeply rooted in devotion and tradition, making it a pivotal pilgrimage site.

Transportation Facilities

Ayodhya is well-connected by road and rail. The nearest airport is in Faizabad, a short drive away. Local transport includes auto-rickshaws and cycle-rickshaws. Click here for bus and train schedules.
